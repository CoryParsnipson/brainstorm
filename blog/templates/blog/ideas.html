About Ideas

Ideas are like "topics" in other blogs, but here they indicate something more serial, with individual posts (called "Thoughts") in each Idea being very closely related.

An Idea is centered around a single ongoing project or line of inquiry that may have a beginning, middle, and end.

This page is a catalog of all the Ideas that I write about on Slacker Paradise.
